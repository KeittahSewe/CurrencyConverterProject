Project Owner: Keittah Sewe
Project Name: Currency Converter

Problem Statement
For many areas, it is difficult to obtain exchange rates for currencies before going to a "bureau de change" to change their money.
They require an apllication that can display up to date exchange rates.

Solution
Creating an application where individuals can acquire exchange rates and convert currencies.

MVP
1. Displaying exchange rates.
2. Converting currencies.
